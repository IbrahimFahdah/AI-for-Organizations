# AI Readiness Framework

## Overview

This framework provides a structured self-assessment of organizational readiness to adopt AI. It is designed for leadership teams initiating an AI strategy and for project teams preparing a specific deployment.

---

## How to Use This Framework

Score each dimension from 1–5 using the descriptors below. Use the aggregate score to identify gaps and prioritize remediation before or alongside AI investment.

| Score | Descriptor |
|---|---|
| 1 | Not present — significant work required |
| 2 | Early stage — partial capability exists |
| 3 | Functional — meets minimum requirements |
| 4 | Mature — well-established capability |
| 5 | Leading — competitive advantage |

## Score Interpretation

| Overall Average | Readiness Level | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|
| 1.0–2.0 | Low | Address foundational gaps before AI investment |
| 2.1–3.0 | Developing | Targeted remediation while beginning low-risk pilots |
| 3.1–4.0 | Ready | Proceed with deployment; monitor identified gaps |
| 4.1–5.0 | Advanced | Scale AI program; share learnings internally |
