**Scoring guide:**
- Strategic Alignment: 5 = directly supports top strategic goal; 1 = no clear connection
- Business Value: 5 = >$1M annual impact or major risk reduction; 1 = minimal impact
- Feasibility: 5 = data ready, talent available, 8-week pilot possible; 1 = significant gaps
- Risk: 5 = low risk, reversible; 1 = high stakes, regulated, irreversible

## AI Adoption Playbook (Per Deployment)

```
Project: _______________________________________________
Owner:   _______________________________________________
Go-live date: __________________________________________

Pre-launch:
  [ ] Success metrics and baseline defined
  [ ] Governance review complete
  [ ] Training materials ready (role-specific)
  [ ] Champions identified and briefed
  [ ] Communication plan approved
  [ ] Rollback plan defined

Launch week:
  [ ] All-hands communication sent
  [ ] Training sessions scheduled
  [ ] Support channel active
  [ ] Monitoring dashboards live

Post-launch (30 days):
  [ ] Adoption rate reviewed
  [ ] Quality metrics reviewed
  [ ] Champion feedback collected
  [ ] Issues triaged and resolved
  [ ] 90-day plan updated
```
