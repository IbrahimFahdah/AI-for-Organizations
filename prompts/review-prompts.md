# Review Prompts

Prompts for technical and editorial review of guide content.

---

## Technical Accuracy Review

```
Review the following technical content for accuracy.

Your role: senior AI/ML engineer with production deployment experience.
Flag: incorrect claims, outdated recommendations, missing caveats, oversimplifications.
For each flag, explain the correct understanding and suggest a revision.

[PASTE CONTENT]
```

---

## Leadership Audience Review

```
Review the following content from the perspective of a VP or C-suite executive
with limited technical AI knowledge.

Flag:
- Jargon that needs to be defined or simplified
- Missing business context or implications
- Sections that are too technical without practical takeaway
- Missing connection to business risk or ROI

[PASTE CONTENT]
```

---

## Legal/Compliance Review

```
Review the following content for legal or compliance accuracy.

Focus on:
- Regulatory claims (EU AI Act, GDPR, sector-specific regulations)
- Liability statements
- Claims about what is "required" vs recommended

Flag anything that may be jurisdiction-specific and presented as universal.
Suggest appropriate qualifications ("in many jurisdictions", "consult legal counsel").

[PASTE CONTENT]
```
